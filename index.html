Instructions

You've been placed in charge of navigating a robot through a maze. Your goal is to move the robot from to the red End space in the fewest moves possible. In order to do that, pass over a Key space to remove the Lock and open the path to the end.

The robot, being a bit primitive, can only do a few things: stop, move forward, and rotate either clockwise or counterclockwise. And since the robot has tunnel vision, it can only perform one of these actions at a time.

Using these features and your understanding of loops, write a sequence of steps that will guide your robot over a key and to the end. Dragging a block will place it behind the block you drop it onto.
